Community Health Outcomes Information Systems (CHOICE)
Welcome to the CHOICE interactive query system. This system empowers users to describe local health department characteristics and community health outcomes and examine interrelationships.
The information is intended to support needs of PH researchers, local health departments, public health policy makers and students of public health systems and services. The primary data sources for this system are National Profile of Local Health Departments (Profile) Data, collected by NACCHO and variety of data elements from the Health Indicators Data Warehouse, developed by HHS.
